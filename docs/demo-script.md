# GBF Demo Script

## 90-second version

1. Open the dashboard and introduce GBF as a Hedera-powered protocol for fractional green bonds.
2. Use the onboarding form to create a new bond program.
3. Point out that the new program is stored in SQL and receives an HCS topic during onboarding.
4. In the operator console, choose a bond and click `Mint FBTs`.
5. Explain that this creates or simulates a real Hedera tokenization flow.
6. Click `Sync dMRV` and show the Truth Stream plus updated bond progress.
7. Click `Schedule coupon` on a qualified bond and explain that yield only unlocks after impact verification.

## Judge talking points

- Access: retail investors can enter with $1 instead of institutional minimums.
- Trust: impact data is live and policy-checked, not buried in annual PDFs.
- Liquidity: bond positions become tradable, fractional units.
- Accountability: coupon distribution is tied to verified ecological performance.
- Hedera fit: HTS for assets, HCS for telemetry, Guardian for methodology enforcement.
