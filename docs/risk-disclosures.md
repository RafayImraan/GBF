# GBF Risk Disclosures

## Technology Risk

GBF depends on Hedera network availability, signer availability, and correct wallet/account configuration. Transaction finality, token association, or scheduled transfers may fail or be delayed.

## Market Risk

Fractional bond positions may lose value, trade at discounts, or remain illiquid even when listed. Secondary-market execution is not guaranteed.

## Policy and Data Risk

Guardian and dMRV checks reduce greenwashing risk but do not eliminate model risk, telemetry errors, satellite blind spots, or bad-source ingestion.

## Compliance Risk

Transfers may be blocked or reversed due to KYC status, jurisdictional limits, sanctions screening, or suitability restrictions.

## Operational Risk

Wallet misconfiguration, lost credentials, signer outages, or treasury mismanagement may affect issuance, settlement, and coupon distribution.

## Not Investment Advice

This software stack does not replace legal, financial, compliance, or custody advice. Any live deployment should undergo full legal and regulatory review before public use.
